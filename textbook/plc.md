### Bit operation  
|Type|Explain|  
|:--|:--|
|NOT|!|
|P|Allow up, 0 to 1|  
|N|Allow down, 1 to 0|  
|S|Set 1 for specific successive bits|
|R|Reset 0 for specific succesive bits|
